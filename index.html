FAQs

Where is my ticket?

Your e-ticket is emailed on confirmation and can be re-downloaded any time from My Bookings. If the email didn't arrive, use the Resend button on the confirmation screen.

How do I cancel and what's the refund?

Open the booking in My Bookings and choose Cancel. Refunds are tiered by how far ahead you cancel: 90% (24h+ before), 70% (12–24h), 50% (4–12h), and none within 4 hours of departure.

How long do refunds take?

This is a demo, so no real money is taken and refunds are simulated. In production, refunds credit the original payment method.

Cancellation & refund policy

90% / 70% / 50% / 0% by time-to-departure, as above. The exact refund for your trip is shown before you confirm a cancellation.
